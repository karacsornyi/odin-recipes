Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is a popular Italian dessert known for its rich layers and exquisite blend of flavors. The name "tiramisu" translates to "pick me up" or "cheer me up" in Italian, which is fitting given its delightful combination of coffee and sweet ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 6 large egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 2 cups strong espresso or coffee, cooled
  • 1 package ladyfingers
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Prepare the coffee mixture
  2. Make the mascarpone filling
  3. Assemble the tiramisu
  4. Chill
  5. Serve
